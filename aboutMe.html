my love for oranges

i developed a deep love towards oranges since the age of 8, when i first tried orange juice. i thought it was so disgusting, so i tried an orange because im stubborn like that, and it was amazing. imagine you as a kid discovering chocolate (if you don't like chocolate, imagine cabbage) for the first time. that was discovering oranges for me.

my favorite oranges are, in order:

  1. mandarin oranges
  2. cara cara oranges
  3. navel oranges
